🏌️ The Core Gameplay: Playing the Field
Instead of playing against a fixed Par, you play against the other participants.
  • The Daily Baseline (E): Every day, the average (median) score of all player lineups is calculated. That average is set as Even (E).
  • Performance Points: If you perform better than the average, you get a negative score (e.g., -2, -5). If you perform worse than the average, you get a positive score (e.g., +1, +4).
✂️ The Cut Line
  • When: The cut happens after the second day (Friday).
  • How: The top 45% of the field (based on the total of your Thu/Fri scores) makes the cut. This includes any ties in score.
  • The Penalty: If you miss the cut, you stop earning performance points for Saturday and Sunday.
🏆 Season Rankings (Points System)
Every tournament finish awards "Season Points" to determine the overall champion:
  • Top 5: 1st (100), 2nd (60), 3rd (45), 4th (35), 5th (30).
  • Top 10: Points scale down from 25 to 16.
  • Top 20: Points scale down from 14 to 5.
  • Finishing the Tournament: Made Cut (3 points), Missed Cut (2 points).
  • WD / DUP: 0 points.
🚫 Fair Play Rules
  • No Duplicates: If your lineup is an exact copy of a groups lineup, you are marked as DUP and receive 0 points.
  • No Withdrawals: If a player withdraws (WD) or fails to submit data, they earn 0 points and do not count as a "start" toward season stats.
